      End of Game

      Game ends if the deck is empty at the end of a turn. The player with the highest ranked person wins. In case of a tie, the player who discarded the highest total value of cards wins.

      Game also ends if all players but one are out of the game, in which case the remaining player wins.

      You can get the rules book here. The following is a summary table.

      Card Rank Card Name Card Amount Card Description
      8 Princess 1 Knocked out if discarded
      7 Countess 1 Discard if the other card you have is King or Prince
      6 King 1 Trade hands with another player
      5 Prince 2 Choose one player to discards his or her hand
      4 Handmaid 2 Protect yourself from other players until next turn
      3 Baron 2 Compare your other card with another player, the person with the lower rank card is knocked out
      2 Priest 2 Secretly look at another player's hand
      1 Guard 5 Choose another player and guess his/her hand, the person is knocked out if you guessed right
